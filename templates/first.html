Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about DBS credit cards.

What are the eligibility requirements for a DBS credit card?

To be eligible for a DBS credit card, you must be at least 21 years old, have a minimum annual income of S$30,000 (S$45,000 for foreigners), and meet our credit assessment criteria. Some premium cards may have higher income requirements.

How long does it take to process my credit card application?

Most applications are processed within 7-10 working days upon receipt of all required documents. If you're an existing DBS/POSB customer with your income credited to your DBS/POSB account, processing may be faster.

How can I check my credit card application status?

You can check your application status online through DBS iBanking or the DBS Digibank app. Alternatively, you may call our hotline at +65 1800 111 1111 for assistance.

What should I do if my credit card is lost or stolen?

If your card is lost or stolen, please call our 24-hour hotline at +65 1800 339 6966 immediately to report it. We will block your card to prevent unauthorized transactions and arrange for a replacement card to be sent to you.

How do I activate my new credit card?

You can activate your new DBS credit card through the DBS Digibank app, via DBS iBanking, or by calling our automated activation hotline at +65 6333 0033. You'll need your card details and some personal information for verification.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our credit cards

What are the eligibility requirements for a DBS credit card?

To be eligible for a DBS credit card, you must be at least 21 years old, have a minimum annual income of S$30,000 (S$45,000 for foreigners), and meet our credit assessment criteria. Some premium cards may have higher income requirements.

How long does the application process take?

Most applications are processed within 7-10 working days upon receiving all required documents. You'll receive an SMS or email notification once your application is approved.

Can I have more than one DBS credit card?

Yes, you can hold multiple DBS credit cards as long as you meet the eligibility criteria for each card. However, your total credit limit across all cards will be subject to your overall creditworthiness.

How do I activate my new credit card?

You can activate your new card through DBS iBanking, the DBS Digibank app, or by calling our 24-hour customer service hotline. Activation instructions will be included with your card.
