Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our credit cards

What are the eligibility requirements for a DBS credit card?

To be eligible for a DBS credit card, you must be at least 21 years old, have a minimum annual income of S$30,000 (S$45,000 for foreigners), and meet our credit assessment criteria. Some premium cards may have higher income requirements.

How long does the application process take?

Most applications are processed within 7-10 working days upon receiving all required documents. You'll receive an SMS or email notification once your application is approved.

Can I have more than one DBS credit card?

Yes, you can hold multiple DBS credit cards as long as you meet the eligibility criteria for each card. However, your total credit limit across all cards will be subject to your overall creditworthiness.

How do I activate my new credit card?

You can activate your new card through DBS iBanking, the DBS Digibank app, or by calling our 24-hour customer service hotline. Activation instructions will be included with your card.
